# Image and Video Panels
Image and Video Panels allow you to import your own images and videos into the studio for 3d manipulation. To access them, go to the “Image & Video Panels” tab on the right hand side of the studio and select either “Image Panels” or “Video Panels” and click “Upload”. 
![tab](tab.png)
![image and video panels](imvidpanels.png)
Once you’ve uploaded your selected media, click on it and drag it onto the scene. You can click to select it and press either T, R or G to adjust position, rotation or scale. Alternatively, you can use the transform panel on the right hand side to adjust it as well. 
![image panels example in scene](panelexample.png)

You can combine multiple image and video panels to create interesting outputs.
