# Create Videos with 3D Objects: A Step-by-Step Guide
Just watched the tutorial? Great! Let's recap the key steps and explore how Storyteller.ai makes 3D video creation a breeze.

{{ youtube(id="zEyiJF6f6NI") }}

## 1. Building Your Scene

- Choose your objects: Just like in the video, start by selecting your 3D objects. We have a wide variety, from office spaces and news anchors (like the ones in the tutorial!) to all sorts of exciting characters and environments
- Position with precision: Use our intuitive controls to place your objects exactly where you want them. Need to fine-tune their positions? Our tools make it simple
- Animate with ease: Want your news anchor to sit down, just like in the tutorial? Our animation features make it effortless to bring your characters to life

To learn more about how to move, rotate and scale objects smoothly in Storyteller.ai, check out our guide on [Becoming a Master of Motion](@/learn/master-motion/index.md)!

## 2. Camera Control

- Capture the perfect angle: Control the camera's position and movement to frame your scene just the way you want it. Experiment with different perspectives and dynamic shots
- Keyframes for smooth movement: Remember those keyframes from the tutorial? They're your secret weapon for creating smooth camera transitions and cinematic effects

## 3. AI Stylization

- 30+ Styles: Explore a wide range of AI styles to give your video a unique look. Go for photorealistic, dreamy, or anything in between!
- Text Prompts: Describe your vision in words, and our AI will bring it to life. Want a specific type of lighting or a particular mood? Just tell us!
- Reference Images: Need your news anchor to have a certain look? Upload a reference image, and our AI will use it to refine the details. Learn more about this in our guide on [How to Control a Character's Face](@/learn/mastering-faces/index.md)

## 4. Generate and Save

- Watch the magic happen: Hit the "Generate Movie" button and watch as our AI transforms your 3D scene into a stunning video
- Save your scene: Don't forget to save your work so you can come back to it later and make any changes you want

## Ready to Create?

With Storyteller.ai, creating amazing videos with 3D objects is easier than ever. Follow the steps from the tutorial, and start bringing your ideas to life!
